Getting to know rumPUREE

rumPUREE is a premier dance studio based in Bangkok, Thailand, known for its inclusive and vibrant community. Established in 2006, the studio helps people of all ages and backgrounds experience dance with confidence.

Located in Bangkok’s central business areas, Asok and Samyan, the studio offers easy access and a wide variety of styles taught by skilled instructors.

Get to know us: rumPUREE

Our philosophy is simple: dance should feel open, professional, energetic, and human. Students come to learn technique, meet like-minded people, and express themselves through movement.
